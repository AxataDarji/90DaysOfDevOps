# DevOps & Cloud Learning Statement

## What is your understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?

DevOps is an engineering culture and operating model focused on **end-to-end ownership, automation, reliability, and continuous improvement** across the software delivery lifecycle—from design and development to deployment, monitoring, and incident response.

A DevOps/Cloud Engineer is responsible for:
- Designing **reliable, scalable, and secure delivery systems**
- Automating infrastructure provisioning and CI/CD pipelines
- Ensuring production stability through monitoring, observability, and alerting
- Troubleshooting failures and continuously improving system resilience

Cloud Engineering enables DevOps by providing **on-demand, programmable infrastructure** using platforms such as **AWS, Azure, and GCP**, allowing teams to build and operate distributed systems efficiently at scale.
